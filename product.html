HFB — Social & Learning Platform

A safe, multilingual, and science-led space for neurodivergent individuals and families.

Polyglot Core — Listen & Translate AI Layer Instant translation for posts, comments, and chats, plus Read‑Aloud (🔊) with natural voices.
Community Spaces Topic-based public channels and private circles with role-based moderation.
Direct & Group Messages E2E encryption, voice notes, files, and reactions.
Knowledge Hub Curated resources (neuroscience, psychology, education) with summaries and bookmarks.
Expert Connect Q&A sessions, workshops, and referrals — privacy-first and opt‑in.
Reflection Journal Capture thoughts and track patterns without overload.
Why HFB? Language and sensory barriers shouldn’t stop anyone from belonging and growing.
