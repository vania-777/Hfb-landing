High Functioning Brains (HFB)

Empowering minds through science and compassion.
Investor Overview
Vision

Empower neurodivergent individuals with privacy-first, science-led tools that translate awareness into action and growth.

Problem

Families, clinicians, and neurodivergent adults lack lightweight, compassionate tools that reduce overload while enabling consistent routines and meaningful progress.

Solution

HFB combines routines, reflection, and optional care-team sharing — designed to be low-friction, humane, and grounded in evidence.

Product (MVP)
  • Daily routines & lightweight check-ins
  • On-device insights, optional encrypted sharing
  • Gentle, non-gamified motivation and streaks
Go-To-Market

Start with a focused pilot cohort; partner with clinicians/educators for distribution; expand via community and referral pathways.

Q2
MVP → Pilot

Why Now

Awareness is rising; tools are either too clinical or too noisy. There’s a need for privacy-first, human-centered design that reduces cognitive load.
