High Functioning Brains (HFB) builds privacy-first tools and verified communities for neurodiverse individuals, families, and clinicians. Structured outcomes data empowers better decisions, care coordination, and long-term independence.

🌐 Clear need

61.8M+ people on the spectrum globally; fragmented tools and data silos for families & clinicians.

🎯 Focused MVP

Private, verified spaces + structured outcomes data for better decisions.

🔒 Privacy-first

Encryption in transit/at rest, least-privilege access, audit logs, opt-in sharing, data minimization.
