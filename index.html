About HFB

High Functioning Brains (HFB) is a neuroscience-driven initiative committed to advancing the understanding and support of neurodiverse individuals through rigorous research, interdisciplinary collaboration, and evidence-based innovation.

Mission

Empower neurodiverse lives by translating evidence into humane, accessible, and private-by-default tools.

Vision

Bridge research and real life—uniting neuroscience, psychology, technology and lived experience.

Values

  • Scientific rigor & transparency
  • Human-centered ethics & privacy
  • Community participation & equity

The Conscious Core

The Conscious Core symbolizes the living intersection of biology, awareness, and connection — a visual centerpoint for how science and empathy converge in HFB’s framework.

It captures HFB’s mission to align research with compassion, transforming data into understanding.

Our Three Pillars

Research Integration

Evidence-based neuroscience and psychology brought together into an adaptive model.

Human-Centered Design

Inclusive tools co-created with neurodiverse participants and verified through ongoing research.

Community Empowerment

Ethical, privacy-first connectivity that turns isolation into shared knowledge and support.
