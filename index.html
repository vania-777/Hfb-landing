About HFB

High Functioning Brains (HFB) is a purpose-driven initiative to connect families, specialists, and neurodiverse individuals through practical, privacy-first tools. We translate the best of neuroscience into everyday solutions—balancing rigor and empathy. Our mission is to empower neurodiverse minds through ethical science and human-centered design.

Science & Technology

Neuroscience

Attention, learning, and sensory models inform brief, modular assessments and self‑regulation tools.

Psychology

Behavioral and developmental insights drive individualized recommendations and progress tracking.

Tech & Privacy

Local‑first data, encrypted sync, and transparent controls—PrivacyFirst by design.
